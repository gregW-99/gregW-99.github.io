My Projects

Vinyl Picture

This is one of the pictures that I have done for my final assignment. I really like listening to music on my record player and I wanted to put it in my final video assignment.

Food and Drink

In this picture, my dad made this meal at the beginning stages of COVID. It is roast beef, potatoes, and asparagus. This meal also really goes well with the wine, as I find any red meat with goes well together like a hand in a gloves.

Character Avatar

This was part of my Digital Graphics mid-term. The goal was to make an avatar for a mobile game. I came up with the idea of an evil robot that destroys the city and earns points based on the amount of destruction they do. I had a lot of fun making this project.

Photoshopped animal

This was also a lot of fun to make. Basically, when we were getting started with Photoshop, our professor basically, the end goal was to take any animal and morph it using several different animals. I got a picture of a wolf and combined it with a dolphin's head, horse legs, and a dinosaur tail. I was laughing while I was making it.

Mid-Term Interview

Here is a video of my partner Pat doing an interview for our midterm. In the video, Pat's mom goes into detail on how her and her husband have met. Pat's parents are from Poland, but they met in Greece. I did all the editing, while Pat did the camera work.

Shaving Video

Here is a video of me shaving with a razor. The premise of the video was to re-create an old film from the early 20th century and re-create it in a modern fashion. In the original video, the subject is having an uncomfortable time trying to shave with his dull razor, while I wanted to re-create that but this time I had a cheap electric razor.
